Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

MyEnglishApp ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account & learning data: When you create an account, we store your email and the words, progress, and statistics you generate while using the app. This data is used to provide and sync your learning experience.

Purchase & subscription data: We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect and process information related to your purchases (e.g., transaction IDs, product identifiers) to verify purchases and restore them across devices. We do not track your behavior beyond what is necessary for these functions.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use advertising networks or analytics that track you across apps or websites. We do not use cookies or similar technologies for tracking.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on our servers and is transmitted over HTTPS. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data through the app (Profile → Delete Account) or by contacting us. If you are in the EEA/UK, you have additional rights under GDPR.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.

Contact
